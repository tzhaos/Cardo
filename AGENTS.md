# AGENTS.md

## Product Copy Constraints

- Do not place explanatory, descriptive, or tutorial-style helper copy in formal product UI.
- Do not add design commentary, implementation notes, or marketing-style text into the interface.
- Use only the minimum necessary product text:
  - titles
  - field labels
  - button labels
  - status labels
  - confirmation text
- Prefer concise product language over guidance copy.
- If a screen works without a paragraph of explanation, do not add one.

## Settings UI Constraints

- Settings pages should look like product surfaces, not documentation.
- Avoid subtitle paragraphs under section titles unless explicitly requested by the user.
- Keep rows compact and action-oriented.
- Theme, Data, and similar pages should expose real controls and state, not placeholder prose.

## Collaboration Rule

- When the user gives a direct product constraint, treat it as a durable repository convention unless they later revoke it.
